Larry David Sandwich

Description

It's got, you got whitefish, you know whitefish. It's got whitefish and sable, they blend very well together. There's some cream cheese. There may be some capers, I'm not sure, if you don't like them you can brush them off it's not a big deal. It's a good sandwich, you're gonna like it.

Ingredients

  • 1 bagel
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons capers, rinsed
  • 1/4 cup prepared whitefish salad
  • 3 to 4 slices smoked sable
  • 3 thin slices white or red onion

Steps

  1. Split the bagel and put on a plate
  2. Spread the cream cheese
  3. Put on the whitefish
  4. Put on the sable
  5. Put on the onion
  6. Put on the capers
