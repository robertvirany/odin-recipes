Snickerdoodle Blondies

Description

These bars are so fire, they are basically cinnamon vannila brownies, highly recommend to all homo sapiens or other great apes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter softened
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granluated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 & 1/4 cup AP flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F then line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper then set aside.
  2. Cream the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Mix in the egg and the vanilla until combined.
  4. Add in the flour, salt, baking powder, cream of tartar, and cinnamon until the flour mixture disappears.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the 3 tablespoon granulated sugar and cinnamon together until combined.
  6. Press the cookie dough in an 8x8 inch baking sheet, sprinkle with cinnamon sugar and bake in the preheated oven for 20-22 minutes. Pull out of the oven and cool down before slicing.
  7. Slice into 16 squares and enjoy!
